MacBook Pro

Apple MacBook Pro is a macOS laptop with a 13.30-inch display that has a resolution of 2560x1600 pixels. It is powered by a Core i5 processor and it comes with 12GB of RAM. The Apple MacBook Pro packs 512GB of SSD storage. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 ac, Bluetooth and it comes with 2 USB ports (2 x USB 3.0), Mic In ports. As of 17th June 2023, Apple MacBook Pro price in India starts at Rs. 159,900.
